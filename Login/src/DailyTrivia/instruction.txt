Welcome to the daily trivia challenge! 
Here are some guidelines to make the most of your experience:


1. You have two attempts to answer each quiz question. 
   For the first correct attempt, you'll earn 2 marks, 
   while the second correct attempt grants 1 mark.

2. It's essential to answer the quiz daily for ten consecutive days 
   to maximize your participation.

3. Feel free to explore the news section to enrich your knowledge 
   before attempting the quiz.

4. Accumulated marks can be redeemed for exciting rewards 
   available at our Point Shop!

5. Remember to check in before diving into the quiz.


Best of luck in your quiz endeavors! 
We wish you an enjoyable and rewarding journey! :)
